Our Story

V&V Fancy Dress and Costumes

V&V Fancy Dress and Costumes is Jaipur's beloved destination for creative and high-quality costumes, conveniently located in Kartarpura. With a vast collection of outfits for school functions, fancy dress competitions, cultural programs, and themed parties, we bring imagination to life for children and adults alike. Our friendly staff takes pride in helping every customer find the perfect look, whether you visit us in-store or coordinate remotely.

Rated 4.3 stars by over 134 happy customers, V&V has built a reputation for beautiful, clean, and diverse costume designs that suit every age and occasion. From tiny tots to grown-ups, our rental and retail options are crafted to make your special moment unforgettable. We are open six days a week and even on Sunday afternoons, ensuring you always have access to the costume of your choice when you need it most.
